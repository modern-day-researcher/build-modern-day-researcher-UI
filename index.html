Welcome To R.ticle!

One stop application that can help with all those issues below....

So many articles, how can I organize?

After reading an article, you can click as "done" and sort by categories for later consumption. Enables people to share and receive articles and information in an organzied manner that they and others find important.

This article was great, how can I send to someone?

All articles you come across can be marked for sharing to someone. Users can log in, receive links, share links and view them on an organzied, filtered interface. You can also see articles others have shared with you.

Is there a way to keep and store article?

All articles can have a mark for completion as well as storing them in various categories. Log in, sign up, user sees page on link shared, filtered links, add links to share and a "mark as read" button/section.
